Course: CS 1428 (Foundations of Computer Science I)
Professor: Becky Reichenau
Rating: 3.5/5

Ngl, I loved being in Becky Reichenau's intro to CS 1. She's a strict grader, but makes it very clear as to what she expects. A lot of people don't like that, but it's what you get in the real world. Plus, she makes sure to break any bad habits that you might have already following her strict style guide. She does do an awesome and thorough job explaining everything in the class.
